About Us

Welcome to New Happy Public School in Bilaspur – a place where learning is fun and exciting!

Our school is not just about books; it's a friendly community where students grow in every way.

At New Happy Public School, we have amazing teachers who really care about students. We want to make sure everyone enjoys coming to school and learns new things every day.

Our classrooms are cool, and we have great places for sports and other fun activities. We believe that learning doesn't only happen in books but also in trying out new things and making friends.

We want our students to be happy, smart, and kind. That's why we have lots of activities that help everyone learn and develop good habits. We celebrate when students do well and help them when they find something tricky.

New Happy Public School is like a big family where everyone helps each other. Come join us, and let's have a fantastic time learning and growing together!

Secondary

Welcome to the Secondary Education program at New Happy Public School, Bilaspur – a stepping stone toward academic excellence and personal growth!

Our Secondary School program, following the CBSE curriculum, is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for the challenges ahead. As students progress through the 10th grade, our experienced teachers guide them with a focus on comprehensive learning, critical thinking, and problem-solving.

We offer a supportive and engaging environment where students not only prepare for board examinations but also develop a deeper understanding of various subjects. Our state-of-the-art facilities, coupled with a dedicated faculty, create an atmosphere conducive to holistic development.

At New Happy Public School, we strive to foster a sense of responsibility, resilience, and a passion for learning, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the next phase of their academic journey. Join us as we navigate through the exciting challenges of Secondary Education and lay the groundwork for a successful future.

Sr. Secondary

Welcome to Senior Secondary Education at New Happy Public School, Bilaspur – a crucial phase where academic excellence meets future aspirations!
